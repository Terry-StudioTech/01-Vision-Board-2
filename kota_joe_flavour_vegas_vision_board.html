Core Concept

The Idea

A glamorous neon entertainment district where milkshakes, burgers, fries, and wings are the celebrities. Marquee glamour meets premium food entertainment.

Why It Works

Kota Joe suits "big event energy." Vegas gives permission to go oversized, lit-up, flashy, bold, and photogenic. Billboard scale is its natural home.

The Look

Main board feels like a luxury casino/hotel sign mixed with diner nostalgia — neon tubing, gold trim, chrome reflections, dramatic spotlight beams, giant 3D letters.

The Brand-Safe Pivot

Not gambling mechanics. Use the aesthetic language of Vegas: lights, spectacle, marquee glamour, jackpot, showtime, luck, indulgence. All feel, zero risk.

Strategic Notes
!

Watch Out — The Risk

If it goes too dark and moody, it loses impact in daytime. The fix: push very bright neon against rich but not muddy backgrounds. Neon always wins over darkness — never let the black swallow the light.

★

The Winning Version

Not "casino." Think: "Las Vegas diner fever dream meets premium food entertainment." Avoid actual gambling mechanics entirely — lean into spectacle, showtime energy, jackpot-winning joy. The food is the jackpot. The meal is the show.
